            <h2>💡 Project Description</h2>
            <p>Infinite Loopers is an interactive web laboratory of absolutely unnecessary technology — designed to entertain, confuse, and waste time productively.</p>

            <h3 style="margin-top:10px;color:#cfe9ff">Features include:</h3>
            <ul>
              <li class="feature"><span class="emoji">👋</span><span>Gesture Control – Wave at your laptop, it waves back (might even open MS Paint).</span></li>
              <li class="feature"><span class="emoji">🎤</span><span>Scream-to-Type – Yell at your computer to make it type nonsense.</span></li>
              <li class="feature"><span class="emoji">🔢</span><span>Useless Math – Solve math problems that have zero practical value.</span></li>
              <li class="feature"><span class="emoji">💗</span><span>Self-Love AI – Compliments you until you’re dangerously overconfident.</span></li>
            </ul>
          </section>

          <section>
            <h2>🤔 The Problem (That Doesn’t Exist)</h2>
            <p>In a world drowning in productivity tools — to-do lists, habit trackers, SMART goals — people are forgetting the joy of wasting time.</p>
            <p>We say: <strong>STOP.</strong> It’s time to embrace creative procrastination.</p>
          </section>

          <section>
            <h2>🚀 The Solution (Nobody Asked For)</h2>
            <p>We created a Useless Project Lab that:</p>
            <ul>
              <li>Tracks hand gestures to open Paint or spam Rickrolls.</li>
              <li>Converts your scream into chaotic, random typing.</li>
              <li>Generates mathematical nonsense that helps in no exam.</li>
              <li>Compliments you until you achieve peak overconfidence.</li>
            </ul>
          </section>
